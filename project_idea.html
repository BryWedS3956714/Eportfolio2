Project Idea

Introduction / Overview

Employment applications can be a tedious process with numerous personality tests, aptitude tests and identification questionnaires. To streamline this process, The Development of a program that serves as a candidate passport that can be provided to employers to gain a general overview of candidate's personality and qualities to assess if they fit the criteria the business is searching for in a certain role.

This program would also be available on the employer's end to ensure that they are provided with a diverse selection of candidates to stop unconscious biases in the hiring process allowing for better workplace diversity.

Motivations

Personal Perspective
The application process can be overwhelming and stressful. Numerous applicants are required to complete a large array of tests, especially when a candidate applies to a large company. The tests themselves only have minor differences and yet candidates are required to take varying tests for each business they apply to. The program will streamline this process as it will present potential employers with the data they need from the candidate, allowing both parties to match their needs effectively.

Business Perspective
A large issue in human resources is an unconscious bias which results in having poor workplace diversity, which in turn can lead to weaker performance and several consequences to a business’s reputation due to a lack of diverse representation in the workforce.

Description

Scramble is a program that acts as an employee candidate passport. The program is designed to be a centralised compilation of a candidate’s requirements ranging from the candidate’s aptitude markings, personality test results, and comprehensive identification. Scramble caters to both employee candidates and employers due to its AI (Artificial intelligence) matching filter that assists in the hiring process.

The fundamental idea of this software is for it to assist and empower both ends of the hiring process. The development of this software will allow a more streamlined process for applying for jobs allowing consumers to apply for more positions at an accelerated rate.

The program will aid Hiring Managers and Human Resource teams in corporations through utilising their systems’ AI (Artificial Intelligence) filtering to give corporations a diverse, non-biased sample of applicants that follow some basic criteria for the role.

The program interface will feature a system that requires the applicant users to input their skills, education, achievements, and work history in the resume section. Afterward, applicants will be assessed through several aptitude and psychological tests such as the Myers Briggs test, Typing Speed and Accuracy tests, and an array of IQ tests. Applicants may also further enhance their profile by testing themselves with additional elective tests such as excel fluency tests.

After the testing has been completed the user will be provided with an ‘Employee Passport’ which will include a mobile QR code. This passport will allow users to apply to employers that take advantage of Scramble and will not be required to fill out a plethora of tests supplied by other employers.

For small businesses, such as cafes, bars, and general stores. They will be able to take advantage of a QR Scanner that will allow people to ‘drop off’ their resume simply by scanning the QR code provided to them via the ‘Employment Passport.’

On the Employer's Interface, businesses will be able to take advantage of the AI filtering system that will automatically select a diverse sample of applicants varying in gender, age, personality results, and behavioural inclinations. Businesses will be able to set minimum requirements in the filtering system with skills that are not discriminatory to ensure that they are getting candidates will have the knowledgeable skills that will allow them to complete the position being required.

The system will also search applicants' resume sections for keywords that will score them higher in the selection process.

This will allow the hiring process for large businesses to be more streamlined and grant them a set of suitable interviewees much faster. Furthermore, using AI systems, will reduce the risk of unconscious bias in the hiring process resulting in a more diverse workforce and a reduction in perceived ageism, sexism, and racism by the public involving the business's operations.

Outcome

In Summary, the development of this program will streamline HR and Hiring Offices procedures and operations allowing for greater business performance and productivity. This will also have great socio-economic benefits for businesses with the previously mention reduction of Unconscious Bias.

Also, for the average individual user, this will allow them to be able to apply for positions and have an increased chance of gaining a first opportunity in the career of their choice as the knowledge of their skills will be more widespread and presented to more businesses for consideration.
